# Kisan Mitra AI - 3 Minute Demo Script

**Speaker Setup:** 
"Hello everyone, we are presenting Kisan Mitra AI, a platform to stop crop failures in India before they happen by giving small and marginal farmers accessible, data-driven agricultural intelligence via voice and SMS."

### Step 1: Smart Crop Recommendation (1 Minute)
*Action: Open the app. In the top selector, choose "Ramesh Kumar (Kangra) - Soil: Loamy". Select "Kharif" season and click "Get Smart Recommendation".*

**Speaker:**
"Our platform is built mobile-first with high accessibility. When a farmer signs up, we build a profile using their soil type and groundwater data. Let's say Ramesh wants to plant for the Kharif season. The engine queries the 7-day rainfall forecast and combines it with his Loamy soil profile."

*Action: Click the "Listen" button on the top recommendation.*

**Speaker:**
"Because heavy rain is predicted, it recommends Paddy. We use the Web Speech API so illiterate farmers can hear the AI's reasoning in their native language directly."

*Action: Switch language to Hindi in the top right corner. Show that the UI translates.*

**Speaker:**
"We natively support i18n, here instantly switching the entire interface to Hindi."

### Step 2: Real-Time Alerts (1 Minute)
*Action: Switch back to English. Click the "Advisories" tab at the bottom. Click the "Trigger Dry Spell Alert (Demo)" button.*

**Speaker:**
"Farming conditions change rapidly. In the background, our alert engine continuously monitors weather APIs. If a 7-day dry spell is detected, it automatically fires an alert."

*Action: Point out the SMS Simulation panel that appears, and the new advisory card.*

**Speaker:**
"The farmer instantly gets an SMS—simulated here—telling them to arrange light irrigation. This ensures they don't lose crops to sudden weather shifts. They can also play this alert as a voice note."

### Step 3: Crop Health & RSK Escalation (1 Minute)
*Action: Click the "Crop Health" tab at the bottom. Click the upload area and select a sample leaf photo (or mock it by just triggering the analysis). Click "Analyze Health".*

**Speaker:**
"Finally, if a farmer notices their crop yellowing, they can upload a photo or record a voice note in their local language. Our AI diagnoses the issue—here, Nitrogen deficiency—and provides an immediate home remedy."

*Action: Point out the red "Escalated to RSK" ticket.*

**Speaker:**
"But importantly, if the AI confidence is low or the disease is severe, the platform automatically routes a structured ticket to the nearest Rythu Seva Kendra (RSK) so human agricultural experts can intervene. This hybrid AI-human approach ensures farmers are never left stranded."

**Conclusion:**
"With Kisan Mitra AI, we bring the power of precision agriculture to every farmer's pocket, regardless of their literacy level or smartphone bandwidth. Thank you."
